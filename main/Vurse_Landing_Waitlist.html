FAQ

Questions worth answering honestly

No marketing fluff. Direct answers to what students actually ask.

Does Vurse use information from the internet or outside my PDFs?

▾
No. Vurse is deliberately constrained to your uploaded materials. Every answer is drawn only from your PDFs — ensuring your sessions reflect exactly what your lecturer taught, not generic internet content.

Can I switch between study modes mid-session?

▾
Yes. Switch modes at any time. Start in Sprint Mode for a quick overview, then switch to Countdown Mode to test yourself. Your uploaded PDFs remain available across all modes.

Is Vurse only for Engineering students?

▾
No — Vurse works for every department. Agriculture, Law, Medicine, Social Sciences, Arts, Engineering — as long as your course has PDF materials, Vurse works with it.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Google's NotebookLM?

▾
ChatGPT answers from general internet knowledge — not your specific handout. NotebookLM does document Q&A but has no concept of your academic goals or Nigerian university context. Vurse combines document-grounded answers with an Academic Context Engine built specifically for students like you.

Will Vurse write my assignments for me?

▾
Scholar Mode helps you write assignments grounded in your course materials — structuring arguments, drafting content, referencing your PDFs. How you use that assistance is your academic responsibility.
